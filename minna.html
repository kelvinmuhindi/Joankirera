An expert in her field, Minaa works with the media to bring education and insight to viewers and listeners on mental health and wellness topics through podcast interviews, TV appearances, news coverage and more. Click below to listen to some of Minaa’s past work.

Based in NYC, Minaa is a contributing writer for Well + Good and XO Necole. She sits on the Mental Health Advisory Committee for Wondermind, a mental fitness company co-founded by Selena Gomez and has also been featured in a variety of publications and media sources, such as Red Table Talk, Peace of Mind With Taraji, Oprah Daily, Katie Couris Media, Today.com, BBC, Essence, Bustle and more.
